Who We Are

Zintro was founded in 2007 to disrupt the existing expert network model by offering the same high-quality participants but with more flexibility and for a lower price.

Today we continue to connect teams with highly specialized respondents, including SMEs, industry specialists, ex-company employees, solutions customers, and more.

What We Provide

No network or panel has everyone. That’s why Zintro uses a
combination of In-Network Experts and Custom Recruiting to find the experts you need. If the ones you’re looking for aren’t already part of Zintro’s professional network, we’ll find them for you.

In Network: 1,000,000+ experts and professionals spanning thousands of specialties across industry sectors, geographies, and job functions.

Out of Network: Our team of recruitment analysts specializes in tracking down niche experts and SMEs on any topic.
